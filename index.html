GPX Chemin

GPX Chemin is a simple web-app to visualize GPX files of sport activities. Leaflet library is used to render the map and Canvas for graph visualization. The files can be loaded from the local drive.

This app is functional and meaningful, it does not just render the gpx track, also display the graphs and tables for speed, elevation, heart rate etc. My plan for the future is to make the app pull activities through 3rd party APIs such as Strava and Decathlon.

This project hopefully showcase my ability to work with JavaScript, XML, Canvas, SVG and basic Express.js (backend).
